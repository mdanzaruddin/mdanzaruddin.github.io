About

I am Mohammad Anzaruddin, a dedicated cybersecurity enthusiast with a B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from Sharda University, specializing in Cybersecurity and Digital Forensics. As the founder of Qubave, I deliver innovative cybersecurity solutions and spearhead research projects addressing contemporary security challenges.
My expertise spans digital forensics, red teaming, threat intelligence, the dark web, and penetration testing. I have actively contributed to the cybersecurity community, serving as the President of the Cyber Pirates Club, where I organized cybersecurity events and workshops. I also provided training on cybercrime investigations, penetration testing, and cyber threat intelligence.
Whenever I get the chance, I provide training on cybersecurity and have previously conducted workshops with law enforcement agencies on cybersecurity.
I am always curious to learn and adapt, staying ahead of evolving cyber threats. Feel free to connect with me at anzarsheikh@protonmail.com. Let's push boundaries and stay curious together.
